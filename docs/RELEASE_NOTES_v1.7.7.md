# Music Digest v1.7.7

Your daily music-intelligence briefing. Every morning it scrapes Reddit, the
charts, and the music press, has AI write you a tight brief on the breaking &
rising artists, builds a Spotify playlist of the day's top tracks, and emails it
to you.

---

## Download

Pick the build for your Mac:

- **Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4)** → `Music Digest-1.7.7-arm64.dmg`
- **Intel** → `Music Digest-1.7.7.dmg`

Not sure? Apple menu  → **About This Mac**. "Apple M…" = Apple Silicon; "Intel" = Intel.

## Install (under a minute)

1. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Music Digest** onto the **Applications** folder.
2. Launch it from Applications or Spotlight — **just double-click**.

That's it. This build is **code-signed with a Developer ID and notarized by
Apple**, so macOS opens it on a normal double-click. No right-click → Open, no
Terminal commands, no Gatekeeper warnings.

## First launch

1. **Sign in with Spotify** for one-tap setup (it also connects your Spotify for
   playlists) — or enter your **name & email** to get the digest by mail. No API
   keys, no payment.
2. Hit **▶ Run Now** for your first digest. After that it runs every morning on
   its own.
